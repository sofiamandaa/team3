Dining007

Our restaurant is about excellent food and tasteful wines and drinks. We wanted to make our restaurant a place where you can relax and have a good time with your friends and family. We wanted to pursue a Mid-European style so that the vibe is fresh and goes along with our menu. We believe that you can’t go wrong with good food and great company.

Premises

The restaurant is a total of 150m2 and it has two different sides: bar/lounge and restaurant. The restaurant is a wide and cozy space, about 90m2 and it is linked to the bar with a small hallway. You are able to move around with wheelchair, our restaurant has an unobstructed passage both in the bar and in the restaurant.

Parties and meetings

You can book our restaurant for an evening, a day or for a whole weekend! There have been many birthday parties; sweet sixteens and celebrations of the big 50, and also bachelor parties and even a few weddings. Or if you want to gather a meeting outside the office with good food and relaxing atmosphere, you can ask for an offer for a private meeting package. Otherwise feel free to book a table from our restaurant for a nice business dinner.
